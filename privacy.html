Physique Tracker Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Physique Tracker may collect the following information from users:

  • Account Information: Email address and login credentials.
  • User Content: Photos, check-ins, and fitness progress data uploaded by users.
  • Usage Data: App performance and analytics data (not linked to your identity).

2. How We Use Information

We use the information to provide, maintain, and improve the app, including:

  • Displaying your photos and check-ins within the app.
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve app features.
  • Responding to support requests.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes. Some information may be shared:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., cloud storage providers).
  • As required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Tracking

Physique Tracker does not track users across other apps or websites.

5. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect user data, but no system is completely secure.
